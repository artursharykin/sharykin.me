HomeLab

Dyno Driveshaft Model

Keywords: Reverse Engineering, SOLIDWORKS

Objectives: Reverse engineer model a driveshaft in SOLIDWORKS to an accuracy of a 0.001".

Summary: A personal project turned work project- this projects purpose was to make an in-house model of a driveshaft used in a dyno cell configuration without having to outsource the work, using a digital caliper & micrometer, each part required precise measurement and referencing old ISO standards for dimension ratios. Due to its shapely design, the SAE 1550 Flange Yoke came out to be the hardest part to design, as measurements provided in correlating documentation came out unclear and lacked required precision, to combat this issue: I traced the outline of the shape, photo scanned and input the file into SOLIDWORKS, using a reference dimension I was able to correctly scale the trace and make a perfect replica of the shape of the flange yoke.
